Äänis Choir is for you
Äänenkannattajat (called Äänis for short) is a laid-back international student choir open to everyone, no auditions! We are affiliated with HYY, the student union of the University of Helsinki, but we admit members from all faculties and schools, and even from outside the university system.

New singer always welcome!

No audition needed, just come.

All we require is an enthusiasm for music and singing! We sing a wide variety of musical styles, from traditional pieces to popular music, and we sing in many different languages (don't worry, we will teach you how!). The rehearsals are instructed in English.
